Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 November 2025

Thank you for using the Zovo App. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Gender (required during signup for profile purposes and app personalization)
  • Password (encrypted in Firebase)
  • UPI ID (used only to process withdrawals and not permanently stored)
  • Device and app information required for basic functionality and ads (AdMob)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage accounts
  • To process withdrawals and payments
  • To send notifications (app updates, payment status)
  • To improve user experience
  • To prevent unauthorized access

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Firebase Authentication and Firestore. Passwords are encrypted, and UPI IDs are stored temporarily and deleted after processing.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data. Limited information may be shared only with trusted service providers for app functionality:

  • Google AdMob (for showing ads)
  • Firebase services
  • Payment processing partners (for withdrawals)

5. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Device and network information
  • Advertising ID
  • App usage and interaction data

Users may see personalized or non-personalized ads. You can disable personalized ads from:

Settings → Google → Ads → Opt-out of Ads Personalization

Note: We do not use Firebase Analytics.

6. Data Retention

  • Data is stored while your account is active.
  • UPI ID is stored temporarily and deleted after payout processing.

7. User Rights

You may contact us to request data removal or account deletion. In-app deletion will be added in future updates.

8. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Updates will be posted with a new date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:
