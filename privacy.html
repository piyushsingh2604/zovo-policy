Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 27 February 2026

Zovo – Quiz & Trivia ("we", "our", or "us") operates this mobile application for users in India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Gender (for profile customization)

b) Account Information

  • Encrypted password (stored securely using Firebase Authentication)

c) Automatically Collected Information

  • Device model and operating system
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Crash reports and diagnostics
  • Advertising ID (Android AD-ID)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To provide quiz gameplay and rewards
  • To personalize user experience
  • To send important app-related notifications
  • To improve app performance and fix issues
  • To display advertisements using Google AdMob

3. Advertising & AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app.

AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is used to provide personalized and non-personalized advertisements.

You can reset or delete your Advertising ID anytime from your Android device settings.

Learn more about how Google uses data:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data.

We only share data with:

  • Google Firebase (authentication and database services)
  • Google AdMob (advertising services)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

5. Data Retention

  • Your data is stored while your account remains active.
  • If you delete your account, your personal data will be permanently removed within a reasonable time unless legally required to retain it.

6. Data Security

We use secure cloud storage and industry-standard security measures via Firebase to protect your data.
