Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 February 2026

Thank you for using Zovo – Quiz & Trivia. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name (required)
  • Email address (required)
  • Phone number (required)
  • Gender (required to display a suitable profile picture)
  • Password (securely encrypted in Firebase Authentication)
  • Device and gameplay information
  • Advertising ID (Android AD-ID) — collected for advertising purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show a profile picture based on selected gender
  • To send quiz rewards or redeem codes via email
  • To send important notifications (quiz updates, rewards, etc.)
  • To improve gameplay experience and app performance
  • To personalize quiz categories and recommendations
  • To display advertisements within the app

3. Advertising & Third-Party Services

Zovo displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Advertising ID (Android AD-ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data

This information is used to provide relevant ads and measure ad performance.

To learn more about how Google uses data, visit:

Google Advertising Policies

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored using Firebase Authentication and Firestore. Passwords are encrypted, and we follow strict data protection practices to keep your information safe.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Limited data sharing occurs only with trusted service providers such as:

  • Firebase (for authentication and secure database storage)
  • Google AdMob (for displaying advertisements)
  • Email services (to deliver reward or redeem emails)

6. Data Retention

  • Your account data remains stored while your account is active.
  • If you delete your account, all related personal data will be permanently removed.

7. User Rights

You may request account deletion at any time.

Delete My Account Request Form
