Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 November 2025

Thank you for playing Zovo – Quiz & Trivia. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you play our game and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Gender (required during signup for profile purposes and game personalization)
  • Password (encrypted in Firebase)
  • UPI ID (used only to process withdrawals and not permanently stored)
  • Device and game information required for basic functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage accounts
  • To process withdrawals and payments
  • To send notifications (game updates, payment status)
  • To improve player experience
  • To prevent unauthorized access

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Firebase Authentication and Firestore. Passwords are encrypted, and UPI IDs are stored temporarily and deleted after processing.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share or sell personal data. Data is only shared with trusted Firebase or payment partners for technical and payment processing purposes.

5. Data Retention

  • Data is stored while your account is active.
  • Temporary data (UPI ID) is deleted after use.

6. User Rights

In future updates, players may get an in-game option to request data changes or deletion.

Currently, our game does not offer an in-game account deletion option.

If you would like to delete your account or request data removal, you can contact us at help.zovoapp@gmail.com and we will process your request manually.

We are working on adding an in-game account deletion option to comply with Google Play policies and provide better control to players.

7. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy. Updates will appear here with a new “Last updated” date.
