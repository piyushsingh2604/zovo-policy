Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 November 2025

Thank you for using Zovo App. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Gender (required during signup for profile purposes and app personalization)
  • Password (encrypted in Firebase)
  • UPI ID (used only to process withdrawals and not permanently stored)
  • Device and app information required for basic functionality and ads (AdMob)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage accounts
  • To process withdrawals and payments
  • To send notifications (app updates, payment status)
  • To improve user experience
  • To prevent unauthorized access

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Firebase Authentication and Firestore. Passwords are encrypted, and UPI IDs are stored temporarily and deleted after processing.

4. Data Sharing

- Google AdMob (for showing advertisements)
We do not share or sell personal data. Data is only shared with trusted Firebase or payment partners for technical needs.

5. Advertising & Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to show ads in our app. AdMob may collect:

  • Device and network information
  • Advertising ID
  • App usage and interaction data

AdMob may use this data to show personalized or non-personalized ads.

If you do not want personalized ads, you can choose non-personalized ads in your device settings:

Settings → Google → Ads → “Opt-out of Ads Personalization”

Note: We do not use Firebase Analytics or collect usage tracking data beyond what AdMob collects for ads.

6. Data Retention

  • Data is stored while your account is active.
  • Temporary data (UPI ID) is deleted after use.

7. User Rights

In future updates, users may get an in-app option to request data changes or deletion.

Currently, our app does not offer an in-app account deletion option.

If you would like to delete your account or request data removal, you can contact us at help.zovoapp@gmail.com and we will process your request manually.

We are currently working on adding an in-app account deletion option to comply with Google Play policies and provide better control to users.

8. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy. Updates will appear here with a new “Last updated” date.

9. Contact Us
