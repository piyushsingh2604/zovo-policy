Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 November 2025

Thank you for using Zovo – Quiz & Trivia. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Gender (required to display an appropriate profile picture)
  • Password (securely encrypted in Firebase)
  • Device and gameplay information
  • Advertising ID (Android AD-ID) — collected by Google AdMob only if ads are displayed in future updates

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display a profile picture based on your selected gender
  • To send rewards and redeem codes via email
  • To notify you about new quizzes, rewards, or updates
  • To improve player experience and app performance
  • To personalize quiz content and recommendations
  • To show ads and improve ad relevance (in future versions)

3. Advertising & Third-Party Services

We currently do not display ads. However, we may use Google AdMob in future versions.

If activated, AdMob may collect:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • App usage data

Learn more: Google Ads Policy

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored in Firebase Authentication and Firestore. Passwords are fully encrypted, and we follow strict data protection standards to keep your information safe.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Limited sharing may occur only with:

  • Firebase services for authentication and database management
  • Email service providers to send reward codes

6. Data Retention

  • Your account information is stored as long as your account remains active.
  • If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

7. User Rights

You have the right to request account deletion or data removal at any time.

Currently, account deletion requests can be made by contacting us at help.zovo@gmail.com.

An in-app delete option will be added in a future update.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Any updates will be reflected here with a revised "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or privacy concerns, please contact us:

help.zovo@gmail.com
