Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 November 2025

Thank you for using Zovo – Quiz & Trivia. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name (required)
  • Email address (required)
  • Phone number (required)
  • Gender (required to display a suitable profile picture)
  • Password (securely encrypted in Firebase)
  • Device and gameplay information
  • Advertising ID (Android AD-ID) — collected by Google AdMob only if ads are displayed in future versions

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show a profile picture based on selected gender
  • To send quiz rewards or redeem codes via email
  • To send important notifications (quiz updates, rewards, etc.)
  • To improve gameplay experience and app performance
  • To personalize quiz categories and recommendations
  • To display ads and enhance ad relevance (in future updates)

3. Advertising & Third-Party Services

Currently, Zovo does not display ads. However, we may integrate Google AdMob in future updates.

If enabled, AdMob may collect:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • App usage statistics

Learn more: Google Ads Policy

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored in Firebase Authentication and Firestore. Passwords are encrypted, and we follow strict data protection measures to ensure your information remains safe.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with any third parties. Limited data sharing occurs only with:

  • Firebase (for authentication and secure database storage)
  • Email services (to deliver reward or redeem emails)

6. Data Retention

  • Your account data remains stored while your account is active.
  • If you delete your account, all related data will be permanently removed from our systems.

7. User Rights

You can request to delete your account and all stored data at any time.

To request account deletion, please fill out this form:

Delete My Account Request Form

Or contact us directly at help.zovo@gmail.com.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updated versions will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
